# COURIER DEMAND INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

## THE FINAL INSIGHT

Saint & Story doesn't get paid for discovering businesses.

Saint & Story gets paid when **something moves**.

Therefore, the system should not be organized around businesses.

It should be organized around **movements**.

---

## WHAT IS A MOVEMENT?

A movement is a physical object that needs to travel from point A to point B within a specific time frame.

**Examples**:

**Property Completion Pack**
- What: Keys + signed completion documents
- From: Solicitor to buyer/seller
- Time: Friday 5pm (deadline)
- Frequency: 2-5 per week
- Revenue per move: £200–£400
- Consequence of failure: Deal falls through (£1000s lost)

**Court Filing Document**
- What: Court bundle + evidence + witness statements
- From: Law firm to court
- Time: Specific filing deadline (no flexibility)
- Frequency: 10–20 per month
- Revenue per move: £200–£500
- Consequence of failure: Case lost (career disaster)

**Emergency Site Part**
- What: Missing component or tool
- From: Supplier to job site
- Time: Same day or crew sits idle
- Frequency: 1–3 per month
- Revenue per move: £100–£300
- Consequence of failure: Project delay (£1000s/hour)

**Medical Specimen**
- What: Blood sample, tissue, test result
- From: Lab to hospital or clinic to lab
- Time: Same day (specimen degrades)
- Frequency: 20–50 per day
- Revenue per move: £40–£100
- Consequence of failure: Test fails (patient health risk)

**The pattern**: 
- Object + Destination + Deadline + Frequency + Cost-of-Failure = Movement Opportunity

## WHY THIS MATTERS

Saint & Story's revenue comes from **movements**, not from businesses.

A business that never moves anything = worthless.  
A business that moves things weekly = valuable.

The system should therefore be built around movements, not businesses.

**Daily Operating Question**:

Not: "How many leads did we discover?"  
But: "How many high-confidence movements did we detect?"

**Daily Sales Question**:

Not: "Who should I call?"  
But: "What movements are happening that I should take over?"

**Daily Revenue Question**:

Not: "What's our company database size?"  
But: "What's our total movement detection value per month?"

---

## CORE INNOVATION

Most courier companies organize databases by **business**.

Saint & Story organizes intelligence by **movement**.

That makes the system:
- More predictable (movements follow patterns)
- More valuable (movements have revenue per unit)
- More actionable (salespeople know exactly what to sell)
- More repeatable (same pitch works for all property completions)
- Harder to copy (intelligence is movement-specific, not business-specific)

---

## THE VISION

**Saint & Story's B2B System**:

Not a lead database.

Not an industry score card.

**A Courier Demand Intelligence Engine**:
- Discovers businesses
- Detects likely movements within those businesses
- Ranks movements by confidence, frequency, urgency, value
- Tells salespeople exactly which movements to pursue
- Generates standing order revenue from identified movements

Every movement represents a potential £500–£5,000 monthly contract.

The system's job is to find movements, quantify them, and tell salespeople how to win them.

That's where Saint & Story's real scaling lever lives.
