# COURIER EVENT OPPORTUNITY ENGINE

## THE FUNDAMENTAL REFRAME

**Old Model** (Industry-Centric):
```
Discover: Estate Agent
  → Look up industry library
  → Recommend: Property Completion opportunity
  → Result: Correct, but inflexible
```

**New Model** (Opportunity-Centric):
```
Discover: Any business
  → Analyze: What needs to move urgently?
  → Detect: Property Completion (87% confident)
  → Detect: Contract Transfer (65% confident)
  → Detect: Urgent Documents (72% confident)
  → Result: Specific courier events, with reasoning
```

**The insight**: Businesses don't buy courier services because they're in an industry.

They buy because **something has to move urgently**.

A solicitor needs court filings. An estate agent needs completion keys. An architect needs revised plans. A lab needs specimens.

**Same action. Different industries.**

The system should organize around the action, not the industry.

## COURIER EVENT LIBRARY

### PROPERTY COMPLETION

**Definition**: Keys and signed completion documents must arrive by deadline (typically Friday)

**Industries that have this**:
- Estate Agents (primary)
- Property Developers
- Conveyancing Solicitors
- Property Management Firms

**Trigger**: Friday property closing deadlines (predictable, recurring)

**Frequency**: 
- High-volume agent: 2-5 per week
- Medium-volume agent: 1-2 per week
- Solo practitioner: 0.5-1 per week

**Urgency**: CRITICAL
- Missed deadline = deal fails
- Buyer and seller both at risk
- Reputation damage

**Revenue Potential**: £1,200–£4,000/month

**Standing Order Probability**: 90%
- Every Friday
- Highly predictable
- Business model depends on it
- Cannot switch providers (once burned, reputation ruined)

**Recommended Action**: CALL TODAY

**Confidence Factors**:
- ✓ Industry is Estate Agent/Property
- ✓ Multiple office locations (key transfers)
- ✓ Google rating 4.5+ (successful closings)
- ✓ Reviews mention "completion," "closing," "keys"
- ✓ Team size 5+ (enough volume)

**Suggested Opening**:
"I saw you handle property completions regularly. Those Friday deadlines are always tight. We specialize in same-day completion support—15-minute driver confirmation so your buyers and sellers know exactly when keys are arriving."

**Why This Works**:
- Addresses their #1 pain point (timing)
- Shows you understand their business (Friday deadlines)
- Offers specific solution (15-minute confirmation)
- Gives them control (real-time tracking)
