### 4. RECOMMENDED FIRST CONTACT METHOD

**Decision Logic**:

**CALL TODAY** (immediate action):
- Trigger event happening within 48 hours
- Time-sensitive decision required
- Relationship building critical
- Examples: Construction emergency, court deadline imminent, completion closing Friday

**VISIT IN PERSON** (relationship building):
- High-value account (£5k+ monthly potential)
- Needs to understand your reliability firsthand
- Service is mission-critical to them
- Examples: Large law firm, hospital lab, major construction firm

**EMAIL SEQUENCE** (nurture):
- Good opportunity but not immediately urgent
- Can establish value through written communication
- Lower priority first contact
- Examples: Small insurance broker, accountant, retail chain

**MONITOR ONLY** (passive watch):
- Moderate opportunity (score 40-60)
- Not yet ready to invest sales time
- Watch for trigger events in their reviews/local news
- Examples: Young startup, new location, unproven need

**IGNORE** (don't contact):
- Low courier dependency (score < 40)
- Different market segment
- No identified trigger events
- Examples: Hair salon, restaurant, small retail

- Probability: 75%
- Reasoning: Emergency parts are unpredictable but frequent
- Trigger: Site emergencies (reactive, not proactive)
- Churn risk: Medium (they have to think of you when problem happens)

**Medical Lab**:

- Churn risk: Very low (chain-of-custody critical, can't switch providers)

```json

  "discovery_data": { ... },
  "opportunity_intelligence": { ... },
  "decision_engine": {
    "primary_opportunity": "Property Completion Keys",
    "trigger_event": "Friday property closing deadlines",
    "likely_delivery_situations": [
      {
        "scenario": "Completion Closing",
        "when": "Friday 4:30pm",
        "what": "Keys + signed documents",
        "urgency": "CRITICAL",
        "frequency": "weekly"
      }
    ],
    "recommended_contact_method": "CALL_TODAY",
    "recommended_pitch": "Same-day completion support. 15-minute driver confirmation...",
    "estimated_monthly_revenue": {
      "low": 1160,
      "high": 3500,
      "currency": "GBP"
    },
    "standing_order_probability": 85,
    "recommended_action": "CALL_TODAY",
    "action_reasoning": "Completion deadlines are Friday. High urgency, high likelihood to convert.",
    "contact_script": "Hi [Name], I noticed you handle about 2 property completions per week..."
  }
}
```

### CALCULATION FLOW

```
Discovered Business
  ↓
[INDUSTRY LOOKUP]
  Identify primary opportunity for their industry
  ↓
[TRIGGER EVENT DETECTION]
  Extract trigger events from business category + reviews
  ↓
[DELIVERY SITUATION MAPPING]
  Map specific delivery scenarios
  ↓
[REVENUE CALCULATION]
  (Frequency × Base Value) × Volume × Multipliers
  ↓
[STANDING ORDER PROBABILITY]
  Frequency × Stability × Our Competitive Position
  ↓
[CONTACT STRATEGY DECISION]
  IF immediate trigger && high revenue → CALL TODAY
  IF high revenue && mission critical → VISIT IN PERSON
  IF moderate opportunity && not urgent → EMAIL SEQUENCE
  IF low signal → MONITOR ONLY
  IF very low → IGNORE
  ↓
[PERSONALIZED PITCH GENERATION]
  Industry template + business-specific details
  ↓
DECISION ENGINE OUTPUT
(stored in JSONB, displayed on dashboard)
```
