## VALIDATION SCORING SYSTEM

For each company, score:

| Metric | Scale | Scoring |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Stage Accuracy** | 0-6 | Did engine identify correct stage? |
| **Trust Score** | 0-100 | Is confidence reasonable? (within ±15 of expert) |
| **Readiness** | 0-100 | Does readiness assessment match reality? |
| **Stakeholder ID** | 0-100 | Did engine identify correct decision maker? |
| **Strategy Logic** | 0-100 | Would experienced person agree with recommended next step? |
| **Objective Clarity** | 0-100 | Is the "why" clear and actionable? |

**Pass Criteria:**
- Stage: Exact or ±1 stage
- Trust: Within ±15 points
- Readiness: Within ±20 points
- Stakeholder: Correct primary stakeholder identified
- Strategy: Logical and expert-endorsed
- Objective: Clear and specific
