## WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN EACH POST

✅ **INCLUDE:**
- Real company with verifiable website
- Full name + job title of poster
- Clear mention of courier/delivery/supply need
- Job posted in last 30-60 days
- Posted recently (not archived)

❌ **EXCLUDE:**
- Anonymous ("Hiring for a client")
- Freelance platforms
- No company info
- No delivery/logistics mention
- Generic HR recruiting

## IF YOU'RE STUCK

**Problem:** Can't find real company website  
**Solution:** Skip that row. Move to next result. Quality > quantity.

**Problem:** LinkedIn only shows "Posted X weeks ago"  
**Solution:** Calculate date backwards. Post today = 7 July 2026. "Posted 3 weeks ago" = 16 Jun 2026.

**Problem:** Post doesn't clearly mention courier/delivery  
**Solution:** Skip. We only want CONFESSIONS of the pain, not implied needs.

**Problem:** Can't find contact name (job posted by HR)?  
**Solution:** Look in comments for hiring manager's name. If not found, skip.

**Problem:** How do I know if a company is "real"?  
**Solution:** Google the company name + "UK". If website + LinkedIn profile exist, it's real.
