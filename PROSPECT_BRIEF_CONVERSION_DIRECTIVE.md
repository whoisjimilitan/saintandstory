# PROSPECT BRIEF CONVERSION DIRECTIVE

**NOT a briefing page. A high-conversion sales intelligence page.**

---

## OBJECTIVE

Current prospect brief output is unacceptable because it is informational.

Reframe: The prospect brief is not a report. It is a conversion vehicle.

The page must inherit both the visual language AND psychological architecture of the homepage.

---

## NON-NEGOTIABLES

### ONE CTA ONLY

- One obvious action
- No competing buttons
- No multiple decision paths
- No dashboard-style options

Visitor always knows exactly what to do next.

### PAIN FIRST

Do not begin with company information.

Begin with the strongest likely operational pain.

Prospect should feel: **"These people understand exactly what we are experiencing."**

Recognition before explanation.

### LESS IS MORE

Remove anything that does not increase conversion.

Every section must justify its existence.

Do not add content because space exists.

### PREMIUM DESIGN

Prospect pages must visually resemble the homepage:
- Same spacing
- Same typography
- Same hierarchy
- Same restraint
- Same emotional tone

Side-by-side with homepage, relationship should be obvious.

---

## SALES PSYCHOLOGY ARCHITECTURE

Current (Wrong):
```
Information
  ↓
Information
  ↓
Information
```

Correct:
```
Recognition
  ↓
Pain
  ↓
Cost of inaction
  ↓
Opportunity
  ↓
Single action
```

---

## MOVEMENTS (Not Generic Cards)

Movements should not appear as informational cards.

Each movement should feel like:
- A missed opportunity
- A hidden revenue leak
- A customer experience problem

Prospect should feel **compelled** to continue reading.

---

## CTA FRAMEWORK

CTA must be framed around highest-probability conversion trigger.

**NOT:**
- "Contact us"
- "Learn more"
- "Get started"

**INSTEAD:** Outcome-driven

### Examples

**Legal:**
"Reduce missed document deliveries"

**Estate Agents:**
"Prevent failed key handovers"

**Medical:**
"Protect time-sensitive collections"

**Construction:**
"Avoid costly site delays"

---

## REPORT REQUIRED

### Audit Current Prospect Brief

Identify every section that weakens conversion.

For each section, explain:
- Why does it exist?
- Should it remain?
- Should it be redesigned?
- Should it be removed?

### Rebuild Accordingly

Apply conversion psychology throughout.

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

A prospect landing on the page should:

✅ Immediately recognize a real business problem  
✅ Believe we understand that problem  
✅ See a clear commercial consequence  
✅ See a clear operational improvement  
✅ See one obvious action to take  

Design quality should be indistinguishable from homepage.

**Conversion quality should exceed current version substantially.**

---

## KEY DISTINCTION

Visual fidelity ≠ Conversion fidelity

Visual fidelity: Looks like homepage  
Conversion fidelity: Converts like homepage

**This directive targets conversion fidelity.**
