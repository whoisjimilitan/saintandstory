## USAGE RULES

✅ **DO**
- Personalize with 1-2 custom lines
- Use operator-approved subject line
- Send from operator email
- Log in CRM before sending

❌ **DON'T**
- Auto-send (manual approval required)
- Deviate from core message
- Send multiple in one day
- Skip logging

---

## CONVERSION TRACKING

After sending, track:
1. **Delivered** - Email accepted by receiver
2. **Opened** - Click-through within 24h
3. **Reply** - Response received
4. **Meeting** - Call/meeting scheduled
5. **Opportunity** - Specific ask made
6. **Revenue** - Standing order created

Log all in CRM for learning.
