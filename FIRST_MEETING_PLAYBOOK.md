# FIRST MEETING PLAYBOOK

**Objective**: Win first meetings from engaged prospects.

**Format**: Category-specific conversation scripts.

---

## ESTATE AGENTS

### Opening
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time. I appreciate you getting back to us. I know you're busy, so I'll keep this brief. We've noticed you're managing relocations for your clients. Is that an area you'd like to improve?"

### Discovery Questions
1. "How many relocations does your business handle per month?"
2. "What's your biggest challenge with the relocation process?"
3. "How are you currently handling it?"
4. "What would an ideal solution look like?"

### Pain Point Exploration
Listen for:
- Client satisfaction issues
- Operational inefficiency
- Time spent managing logistics
- Complaints or friction

### Offer Presentation
"What we do is partner with estate agents to streamline relocations. Our operators handle the logistics, your clients feel supported throughout, and you save time. We've worked with similar agents and they've seen better client retention and faster sales cycles."

### Meeting Outcome
**If interested**: "Would it make sense to do a quick pilot with 2-3 of your next relocations?"

**If hesitant**: "What would it take for you to consider trying this?"

## UNIVERSAL CLOSING

**Regardless of category**:

"Here's what I'm thinking — let's do a pilot with your next 2-3 [relocations/events/customers]. We'll handle everything, you'll see how it works, and we can decide from there. Does that make sense?"

**If yes**: Schedule pilot start date

**If no**: "What would need to change for you to try it?"

---

## MEETING SUCCESS CRITERIA

✅ **Prospect agrees to pilot**
✅ **Next action scheduled**
✅ **Timeline established**

❌ Do not claim success if prospect only "seems interested" — pilots must be committed.
